View/generate my CDS manifest

You use the View/generate my CDS manifest function for producing a manifest report containing your CDS usage and installation details. You need to provide this report to the PTC for any support-related questions, and as part of the volume-based yearly license agreement.

To generate a manifest

  1. Click Generate manifest. This creates the CDS manifest in the Content area of your screen. You can select the text and copy it to another application if required.
  2. Click Send to UPU (if required). This automatically prepares email content and a subject line that you can send to the PTCâ€™s support or finance team. For this option to function correctly you need an email client installed on your workstation.
  3. Click Close to return to the CDS Home page.
