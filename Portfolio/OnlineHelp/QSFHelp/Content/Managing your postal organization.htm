Managing your organization

To manage your organization (also known as stakeholder in QSF), click the tab showing your organization's name on the Home page. Using this function, you can:

  • View stakeholder details
  • View and add new contacts to the stakeholder
  • View the history of the groups your organization has belonged to, by year
  • View and add bilateral agreements

Procedures

Viewing stakeholder details

You can view details of your stakeholder in the section of the page under the icon. This section is read-only.

Adding a new contact to the stakeholder

  1. Click the icon in the section of the page under the icon.
  2. In the Contact Information tab, complete the details of the contact. All fields with a red asterisk are mandatory.
  3. In the Privileges tab, check the application role(s) of the contact.
  4. When you have finished updating the contact, click Save. The new contact is added to the stakeholder after it is validated by the UPU.

Viewing your stakeholder's group history

You can view the history of your stakeholder's terminal dues group membership by year in the section of the page under the icon. This section is read-only.

Adding a new bilateral agreement

  1. Click the icon in the section of the page under the icon.
  2. Complete the details of the bilateral agreement. All fields with a red asterisk are mandatory. Some fields are completed for you, so you need to check and update them.
  3. Attach a signed paper agreement, if you have one, by clicking Signed Agreement in the Agreement details section.
  4. When you have finished updating the bilateral agreement, click Save.
